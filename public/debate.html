Massivi Al-Xorazmiy 1-chi, 35

The Debate Club at School 126 provides students with an excellent platform to hone their public speaking and critical thinking skills. Through engaging debates on various topics, members learn how to present compelling arguments, develop persuasive communication techniques, and think on their feet. The club fosters a supportive and competitive environment, encouraging students to explore different perspectives and sharpen their analytical abilities.

In addition to enhancing speaking skills, the Debate Club helps students build confidence and teamwork. Members work together to research topics, structure arguments, and anticipate counterpoints. Participating in debates not only strengthens their academic abilities but also prepares them for real-world discussions and decision-making, making it a valuable experience for all involved.
