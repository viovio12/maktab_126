Massivi Al-Xorazmiy 1-chi, 35

An IELTS club is a group where members come together to prepare for the IELTS (International English Language Testing System) exam. This club was created by the vice president for students who want to apply to foreign universities and improve their English proficiency. The club focuses on helping students enhance their skills in the four key areas tested in the exam: listening, reading, writing, and speaking.

In an IELTS club, members often practice sample exam questions, engage in mock speaking tests, and share tips and strategies for improving their scores. There may be group discussions, essay writing workshops, and listening exercises to simulate the actual test environment. Members also support each other in areas they find challenging, offering peer feedback and motivation. The club provides a structured, collaborative way to prepare for the IELTS exam while improving overall language proficiency.
